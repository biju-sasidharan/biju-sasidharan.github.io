Insights about me

I'm Biju, a data scientist by profession, who loves to get hands dirty with data. I get excited by seeing data helping businesses to make better and informed decisions.


Years back, when I started my career in this industry, I was fortunate enough to get into projects that required analysis of huge amounts of data. From then, there is no looking back, analyzing and troubleshooting data has become a day-to-day activity. With time, identifying patterns in data has become an involuntary skill.


I, currently work as a Data Scientist at Tech Mahindra. My work mainly revolves around developing the first hand business ideas around what to measure, capture relevant data, prepare, analyze and identify meaningful insights.
